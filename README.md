# Superstore Sales Analysis (SQL - BigQuery)

## Overview

This project analyzes a retail sales dataset using SQL in BigQuery to understand business performance, profitability trends, customer behavior, and sales patterns.

Rather than focusing only on revenue, the analysis explores the factors that influence profitability and overall business performance.

---

## Key Questions

* Which products generate high sales but result in losses?
* Do higher discounts improve or reduce profitability?
* Are high sales always associated with high profit?
* How do customer purchasing behaviors differ?
* Which cities perform best in each region?
* How do sales trends change over time?

---

## Key Findings

* Several products generate high sales but still produce negative profit margins
* Higher discount levels are generally associated with lower profitability
* High revenue does not always indicate strong business performance
* Customer purchasing behavior varies significantly across segments
* Top-performing cities differ between regions
* Sales fluctuate over time, indicating changing monthly trends and possible seasonal patterns

---

## Business Insights

The analysis suggests that:

* Revenue alone is not enough to evaluate business success
* Excessive discount strategies may negatively impact profit margins
* Product profitability should be monitored alongside sales performance
* High-value customers play an important role in overall revenue contribution
* Regional performance differences may require targeted business strategies
